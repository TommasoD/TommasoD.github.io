About Me

I am a PreDoc Researcher in the DMKI Lab at TU Wien and a Marie Skłodowska-Curie doctoral fellow within the ARMADA project. My work primarily focuses on LLM hallucinations, factuality evaluation, and knowledge graph grounding. I am an advocate of Digital Humanism, the initiative to preserve human agency in digital technology.
