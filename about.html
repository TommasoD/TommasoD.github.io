About Me

Bio

Tommaso is a PreDoc Researcher in the Database and Artificial Intelligence Unit at TU Wien (Austria).
He is also a Marie Skłodowska-Curie Doctoral Fellow within the ARMADA (EU Horizon-MSCA project).
He received his MSc in Computer Science and Engineering from Politecnico di Milano with a thesis on social bias in language models .

He has been Post-Graduate Researcher at Lero (Irish Research Centre for Software) in the SPARE team, and Research Assistant at Politecnico di Milano (Italy) in TISLab and DEEP-SE Lab. He also worked as a Responsible AI Engineer at Accenture R&D Unit - RAI Center of Excellence.

He has served as a reviewer for workshops, conferences and journals in the fields of databases, data management and software engineering: CIKM'24 (Demo); WALS'23; CMLS'24, '23; ICTAI'23; TOSEM; HIJ.
He served as co-supervisor of 10 MSc theses at Politecnico di Milano and teaching assistant for BSc and MSc courses in software testing, databases, and computer science fundamentals at Politecnico di Milano and the University of Limerick (Ireland).
